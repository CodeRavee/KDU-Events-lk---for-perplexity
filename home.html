ABOUT THE SYSTEM

The KDU EVENT.LK is designed to provide a central platform where students can easily find, register, and stay updated about upcoming university events. It also supports event organizers by offering tools to publish and manage their events in a more structured way. An important feature included is the "Request Event Permission" option, which allows organizers to conveniently apply for approval of their events through the system.
